Our Story

Founded in 1995 by Italian immigrants Giuseppe and Maria Rossi, Slice of Heaven brings authentic Neapolitan pizza to your table. Our commitment to quality ingredients and traditional techniques has made us a local favorite for over 25 years.

We source our ingredients from local farmers and import authentic Italian tomatoes and mozzarella di bufala to ensure every pizza tastes like it came straight from Naples.
